About Our Program

This workshop recognized the urgent need for culturally sensitive, comprehensive support services. Many migrant women working in domestic labor and low-wage positions face isolation, unsafe working conditions, and limited resources for self-care.

Our program was born from direct conversations with women like Nadi from Sri Lanka, who told us "This is the first day off I have had in months," and Lakshmi from India, who shared "I feel very alone and sometimes I just want to talk to someone." These voices shaped our mission.

Through this workshop, emergency support networks, and community building activities, we ensure that no woman faces these challenges alone. Our program combines safety education, emotional wellness training, and meaningful social connections.

Our Mission: Empowering Through Support & Education

Our mission is to empower migrant women working in the UAE by providing support, education, and practical tools that promote their safety through awareness of their legal rights at work and home, and practical tools to ensure emotional well-being.

Feeling Safe & Protected

"Us women want to feel we are safe when we are working so we can do our jobs well. If we don't feel safe in our working environment, it's difficult to focus and be productive. Our families count on us." - Workshop Participant

Self-Care & Stress Relief

Long working hours leave little time for personal care. We provide practical tools for daily self-care including breathing exercises, wellness journals, and stress management techniques that fit into any schedule.

Meaningful Friendships

With families far away, quality friendships become essential. Our program creates safe spaces for sharing experiences with other women who understand the unique challenges of working in the UAE.

Supporting Migrant Women in the UAE

A comprehensive monthly workshop series held on one Friday each month, designed to ensure safety, promote self-care, and build strong connections among migrant women working in the United Arab Emirates

Month 1: Foundation Building

125 minutes
Learning Goals
Legal Rights & Safety Awareness

Learn comprehensive worker rights in the UAE, including working conditions, living accommodations, medical care, and passport handling. Recognize signs of abuse and trafficking.

Self-Care & Emotional Well-being

Develop daily self-care practices including guided meditation and breathing exercises to manage stress in challenging work environments.

Building Supportive Connections

Combat isolation by forming meaningful relationships with other expat women who share similar experiences and challenges.

Activities
Visual Learning Presentation (15 min): Clear, multilingual PowerPoint covering legal rights, safety protocols, and abuse recognition using simple language and visuals.
Knowledge Assessment Quiz (10 min): Interactive quiz to ensure understanding and build confidence in applying safety information.
Emergency Contact Setup (10 min): Personalized contact cards with Dubai Foundation for Women and Children (888-111) and Police (999) numbers programmed into phones.
Guided Meditation Session (15 min): Learn breathing techniques and mindfulness practices for stress management and emotional regulation.
Wellness Journal Distribution (15 min): Personal journals for tracking emotions, gratitude, goals, and self-care practices with guided instruction.
Small Group Connections (45 min): Facilitated get-to-know activities and personal story sharing to build understanding and support networks.
Contact Exchange (15 min): Exchange information with nearby participants to establish ongoing support relationships outside the workshop.

Month 2: Safety Planning & Reporting

115 minutes
Learning Goals
Personal Safety Planning

Create comprehensive, personalized safety plans with step-by-step actions for emergency situations and abuse reporting.

Job Security & Support

Address fears about job loss when reporting grievances and learn about guaranteed placement support from authorities.

Comprehensive Abuse Recognition

Identify all forms of abuse, neglect, and rights violations through expert-led discussions and interactive scenarios.

Activities
Safety Plan Worksheet (30 min): Complete personalized emergency action plans including contact information, safe locations, and step-by-step reporting procedures.
Role-Playing Scenarios (10 min): Practice emergency calls using "safe words" and communication techniques with police, Dubai Foundation, and trusted contacts.
Open Discussion Forum (20 min): Facilitated conversation allowing participants to voice fears about job loss and receive professional guidance.
Police Officer Assurance Session (25 min): Direct interaction with law enforcement to build trust and learn about job placement support services.
"Is This Abuse?" Interactive Activity (40 min): Room-wide poster analysis of various scenarios to distinguish between acceptable and abusive behaviors with expert feedback.
